Privacy Policy

At ViDrive, your privacy is our priority. We are committed to being transparent about the data we collect and ensuring that your information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

What Data We Collect

1. Opt-in Analytics Data

We only collect analytics data if you opt-in to share it with us. This data includes anonymized and aggregated application usage information such as:

  • First app open
  • Login events
  • Notifications opened while the app is active
  • Screen views within the app
  • Session start times
  • App errors and crashes
  • Operating system updates

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally. It helps us improve the app's functionality and user experience.

2. Email Address

We collect your email address to support essential features, including:

  • Account authentication
  • Password resets

How We Handle Your Data

Anonymized Analytics

The analytics data we collect is aggregated and stripped of any information that could identify you or your device.

No Selling or Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for any purpose.

Your Control

You have complete control over whether or not you share analytics data with us. By default, no analytics data is collected unless you explicitly opt-in via the app settings.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@vidrive.com.

By using ViDrive, you agree to this Privacy Policy and our commitment to safeguarding your information.
